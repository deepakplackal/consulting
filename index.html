PVA

Antony P.V.

ACS · FCMA

P. V. Antony is a seasoned finance and governance professional with over four decades of experience in banking, corporate finance, risk management, and regulatory advisory. He currently serves as Head – Finance at ESAF Small Finance Bank and has previously held senior leadership roles including Chief Financial Officer and Chief Risk Officer at Catholic Syrian Bank (CSB Bank).

Throughout his career, he has been closely involved in capital planning, regulatory compliance, risk management, financial strategy, and large-scale institutional initiatives including the bank's IPO process.

A qualified Company Secretary (ACS) and Fellow Cost & Management Accountant (FCMA), Mr. Antony has consistently combined deep financial expertise with strong governance insight. He has also contributed to industry-level initiatives, including serving on the RBI Working Group on the implementation of IFRS in Indian banks and participating in banking industry sub-groups on accounting, taxation, and risk management.

Through Antriom Advisory, he brings this extensive institutional experience to support businesses in areas such as financial strategy, capital structuring, governance advisory, and business restructuring.
